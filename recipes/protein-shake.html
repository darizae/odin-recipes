Protein Shake Recipe

Description

If you're goal is to build or maintain muscle mass, a high protein diet is a must. Getting the necessary daily amount of protein, however, can be difficult.

Protein powder is an easy way to achieve you daily protein intake goal. Not only is a protein milk-shake tasty and protein-rich, you also add other ingredients to increase these properties and achieve a higher calory count just by drinking something.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 30 g of oats
  • 25 g of peanut butter
  • 300 ml of milk
  • 25 g of protein powder

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and put it in the flask
  2. Add oats
  3. Add milk
    • Adding the milk before the PB and the protein powder prevents them from sticking to the flash walls
  4. Add the PB however you like
  5. Add protein powder
  6. Mix it all with the mixer

And enjoy!
